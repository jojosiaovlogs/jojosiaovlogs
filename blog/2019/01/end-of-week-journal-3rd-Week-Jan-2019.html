I am writing this so I won’t forget these things that I need to do.

This is to remind my future self that my past self wants him to change for the better. My past self is claiming that my past self has been having this “loser”-mindset and he wants to stop it.

So it is expected from my future self that he has done these things to improve himself:

  1. Show the world what you love. Blogging, Ruby and Java, Chess, PC games, Nintendo Switch, etc.

  2. Go back to meeting new friends, attend events and do some networking again.

  3. You must change that “loser” mindset. NOW.

Your past self is hoping that by the end of this year, 2019, you’ve accomplished these things.
