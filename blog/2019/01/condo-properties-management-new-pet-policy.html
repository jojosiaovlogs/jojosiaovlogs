Condo Admin Enforces Pet Policy

Date Published: 24 Jan 2019

  1. Home
  2. My Blog Posts
  3. Condo Admin Enforces Pet Policy

Hey, Future Self, this is your Past Self writing to you!

I just want to know what happened to the pet policy they are going to strictly enforce. They are enforcing the rule that every tenant or Condo unit owner can only have 1 pet regardless of size. I just find it so hard to follow this crazy rule because, I am confused if they are going to prohibit us to even own more than 1 goldfish in an aquarium, or just 1 hamster? Are they going to be biased on prohibiting only having dogs, just because they seem scary with all the sharp teeth or barks very loud?

I am also concerned about how they will be able to enforce this rule considering that within the condominium’s premises, there are a lot of stray cats and they are sometimes causing loud sounds sometimes at night.

I am just confused right now because I don’t know how am I going to be able to follow this unfair rule. I just love my 2 chihuahua. I love Ruby and I also love Java. And Mama also love them both when she was still alive. They make Mama happy.
